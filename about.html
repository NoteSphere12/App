About Noteca

Noteca is a platform created by students, for students — offering free, high-quality handwritten notes for JEE Mains, Advanced, and other PCM-related exams. We believe that learning should be accessible and clear, and our mission is to make it easier for students to revise and master concepts using real, clean, and concise notes.

We crowdsource, organize, and curate handwritten notes from top students and educators. Whether you're just starting your preparation or brushing up on tough topics, Noteca is designed to simplify your journey.

Our Vision

We envision a world where students from all backgrounds have equal access to the best academic resources — completely free. We're continually improving Noteca to ensure it remains useful, beautiful, and efficient.
